How To Play

MOBILE: To play on your mobile device, all you need to do is use your device gyroscope sensors to move your ship around space. Tap on the screen to launch missiles and tap and hold with two fingers to activate your ship's protective shield.

DESKTOP: move the ship around with the arrow keys on your keyboard. Use the down arrow to activate your ship's protective shield and press "Space Bar" to launch a missile. You can pause the game by pressing "P"
